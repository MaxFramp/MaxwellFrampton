Maxwell Frampton

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Maxwell Frampton. I am an aerospace engineer, currently working for the United States Air Force as an intern through the Palace Acquire internship program. I graduated cum laude from the University of Alabama in May 2023. Since then, I have been working passionately to start my career.

With a foundation built on academic rigor, I am dedicating myself to excellence in my work and pursuing both professional and personal projects with the same passion that has defined my academic career. In the short time since I began working for the USAF, I have advanced far beyond the old horizons of my education.

Right now I am preparing for the next steps in my career by pursuing a graduate degree in computer science. My undergraduate studies helped me gain foundational skills in Python and C++. In my time working for the USAF, I have developed those skills far beyond where I started, and have gained experience with C#, SQL, Fortran, and a wealth of other important skills.
