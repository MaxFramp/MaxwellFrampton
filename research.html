Research

Lab for Computational Imaging and Smart Structures (CISS)

In October 2021 I joined the CISS Lab, led by Dr. Jiaze He, a professor in the Department of Aerospace Engineering and Mechanics at The University of Alabama. Some of the responsibilities and learning opportunities that I have had through this experience include:

  • Devising and implementing cutting-edge ultrasonic imaging methods for structures, with applications in nondestructive testing
  • Employing the Verasonics Vantage research ultrasound system to measure propagation of ultrasonic waves through experimental specimens
  • Creating and executing MATLAB scripts to analyze, interpret, and visualize data
  • Developing and modeling experimental specimens deploying SolidWorks

    • My time working in the CISS Lab with Dr. He has been a wonderful experience. I have greatly developed and enhanced my coding skills, and have learned a huge amount about digital signal processing, and the potential applications for it in the aerospace field. I have also had a great chance to develop greater analytical and experimental skills, which have already yielded great benefits, both in professional and in academic settings.
